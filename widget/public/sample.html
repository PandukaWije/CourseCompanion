üì¶ How to Integrate

Integrating CourseCompanion into your website is incredibly simple. Just add two script tags before the closing </body> tag: 

<!-- CourseCompanion Widget -->
<script src="https://cdn.yoursite.com/coursecompanion-widget.js"></script>
<script>
  window.CourseCompanion.init({
    userId: "user_12345",
    apiUrl: "https://api.coursecompanion.com",
    theme: "light",
    position: "bottom-right"
  });
</script>
